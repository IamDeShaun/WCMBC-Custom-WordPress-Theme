Who We Are

Welcome Chapel Missionary Baptist Church was established in 1957 at its current location of 124 Chestnut Street in the Capital Region. Since its inception God has blessed its membership and the Pastor. Since the passing of Rev. Robert Earl Graves, after 27 years of service, Welcome Chapel members and officers have been in prayer and meditation for God's direction. God will answer prayers. After much deliberation and support from the officials of the local association, Hudson River Frontier Missionary Baptist Association, under the leadership of Rev. Dr. Elgin Joseph Taylor Sr. as Moderator, Rev. Victor Covington, Vice Moderator, and Rev. William E. Morgan, Area-Vice President of the Empire Baptist Missionary Convention of New York, Inc.;

Welcome Chapel members and officers prayerfully entered into the voting process for a new Pastor.What God had for us, was for us.After a vote on the recommendation from Pastor Graves prior to his passing; Welcome Chapel entered into the process of voting for the Pastor and on DECEMBER 5, 2015; the members and officers, under the facilitation of Rev. Dr. Elgin Joseph Taylor Sr. and Rev. William E. Morgan, proceeded to vote to close the process and vote on the ONE candidate presented before the body.The vote was UNANIMOUS; Rev. Constance D. Knight was elected Pastor of Welcome Chapel Missionary Baptist Church. Rev. Dr. Taylor called Rev. Knight on the phone and proceeded to seek her response to the vote of the membership. And she accepted.Pastor Knight has poured out to God's people and he has answered our prayers. As she would say, "To God be the Glory."
